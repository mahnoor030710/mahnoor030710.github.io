Portfolio Project 2

AMS Website - Redesign

I undertook a comprehensive redesign of the AMS website to enhance its layout and user interface, aiming to create a more modern, visually appealing, and user-friendly experience. Leveraging the sophisticated color palette of Amazon, I ensured a cohesive and professional appearance throughout the site.

The redesign features a clean, contemporary layout that is both easy to read and navigate, significantly improving user engagement and satisfaction. Key enhancements include an advanced navigation bar, making it intuitive for users to find what they need quickly and efficiently.

Additionally, I integrated numerous icons to provide visual cues, enhancing understanding and accessibility for all users. This approach not only makes the site more visually appealing but also aligns with modern design standards. Overall, the redesign has driven better user engagement and satisfaction by making the website more accessible and user-friendly.
