Portfolio Project 5

Promotional Material for Lakeshore Ground's Historical Event

During my internship at Lakeshore Ground Interpretive Centre, I was part of a dedicated team tasked with promoting the Centre's historical tours through various print materials. Our aim was to adhere to Lakeshore Ground's brand values, strategies, and color palette while creating more attractive and engaging posters to draw in a diverse audience, primarily students and tourists..

We started by redesigning the brochure, ensuring it aligned with Lakeshore Ground's brand colors and values. The new brochure is visually appealing, easy to read, and effectively showcases the key attractions and historical significance of the interpretive centre.

In addition to the brochure, we developed a range of vector-based advertising materials, including posters. Our goal was to make these materials more fun and engaging to better attract students and tourists.
