Portfolio Project 2

Wedding Invitation Video

For this project, I created a wedding invitation video for my wedding. The video is rich in vector graphics and features a gold, green, and red theme, matching both my outfits and the overall wedding theme. The design reflects my personality, showing my preference for aesthetic light themes as well as my appreciation for darker colors such as maroon. The theme also highlights the colors of my wedding dresses: red and gold.

Regarding the design elements, I used a prominent, heavy yet playful font like "Blanket" for the headings, dates, and venue name. I paid careful attention to the hierarchy by making the dates and venue name bold to ensure they stand out.

Technology

  • Illustrator
  • After effects
  • Photoshop
