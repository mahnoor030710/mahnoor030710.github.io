Portfolio Project 2

Webside Design Craigslist

For this portfolio piece, I redesigned the website for Craigslist, focusing on enhancing the user experience while maintaining the core purpose of the site: making listings. I adhered to Craigslist's brand strategy to ensure continuity and familiarity for users but modernized the appearance to be more appealing and user-friendly.

The redesign features a clean, simple layout that avoids over-complication, ensuring that users can easily navigate the site and create listings without confusion. To achieve a modern look, I incorporated subtle design effects and used clean, non-edgy lines to maintain simplicity while adding a contemporary touch. The interface is intuitive, making it easy for users to find what they need quickly and efficiently.

Overall, this redesign aims to retain Craigslist's trusted functionality while providing a fresh, modern user experience that appeals to a broad audience.
